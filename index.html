WHO ARE WE?

The European Association of Geoscientists and Engineers (EAGE) is a professional association for geoscientists and engineers. Founded in 1951, it is an organization with a worldwide membership (with members active in over 100 countries and territories). The student chapter of this organisation in the Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur was established in January 2024. We believe that this initiative will enable students at IIT-K to gain more industry exposure. What can you expect?

  • Stay informed: We'll keep you in the loop with the latest industry news, trends, and updates.
  • Network and collaborate: Connect with like-minded professionals, share your insights, and foster meaningful collaborations across this and other student chapters.
  • Engage with us: Your thoughts matter! We encourage active participation and discussions on our posts. We will also conduct workshops and live events to promote participation in geosciences.
